Deep Cleaning

This is our signature service, a top-to-bottom, detail-oriented, rewarded cleaning. Our team will go to all the hidden spots that we don’t notice on a daily basis. This service also includes the inside of your appliances, inside of all reachable windows, and inside of your cabinets if your home is empty. For this service, we usually send a team of 4 cleaners but this number can vary and the amount of time we spend will depend on each individual project based on the property size, house organization, and actual cleaning conditions. 

Benefits of Deep Cleaning

  • Removes dirt, grime, and bacteria from hard-to-reach areas.
  • Improves indoor air quality by eliminating dust and allergens.
  • Restores the appearance and freshness of the space.
  • Helps prevent mold and mildew growth.
  • Ideal for thorough cleaning on a periodic basis.
